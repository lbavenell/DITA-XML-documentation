How to Divide Numbers

Here you will find instructions for using your calculator app. to divide numbers.

To successfully divide numbers, you must understand division.

  1. Press the first number's button.
  2. Press the (รท) button.
  3. Enter the second number's button.
  4. Press the (=) button.

The result from the division is displayed.

Example:

You want to divide 4 by 2. The equation will look like 4รท2=2. Press the 4 button. Press the division symbol (รท) button. Press the 2 button. Press the equals sign (=) button.

The result, the number 2, will appear on the display.
