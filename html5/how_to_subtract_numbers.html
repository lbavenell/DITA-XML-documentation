How to Subtract Numbers

Here you will find instructions for using your calculator app. to subtract numbers.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must understand subtraction.

  1. Press the first number's button.
  2. Press the (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number's button.
  4. Press the (=) button.

The result from the subtraction is displayed.

Example:

You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation will look like 3-2=1. Press the 3 button. Press the minus (-) button. Press the 2 button. Press the equals (=) button.

The result, the number 1, will appear on the display.
